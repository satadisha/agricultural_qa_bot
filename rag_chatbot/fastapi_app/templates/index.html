Seedbot: Q&A Tool for Farmers

Welcome to the Q&A bot for agricultural relations.

SeedBot was created in collaboration with A Growing Culture, a non-profit organization focused on advancing global food sovereignty. We built the platform using Microsoft's open-source large language model, phi-4, and trained it on the International Union for the Protection of New Varieties of Plants' dataset of agricultural laws. Our goal is to empower farmers with free and easy-to-understand access to laws and regulations related to agriculture.
