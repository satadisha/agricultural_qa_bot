Seedbot: Q&A Tool for Farmers

Welcome to the Q&A bot for agricultural relations

In collaboration with A Growing Culture, a non-profit organization focused on advancing global food sovereignty, we used the International Union for the Protection of New Varieties of Plants' dataset of agricultural laws to build this Q&A chatbot. Our goal is to empower farmers with easy-to-understand access to laws and regulations related to agriculture. Built using open-source large language models (LLMs), ensuring the platform remains completely free for users.
